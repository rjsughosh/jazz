Create. Manage. Self-Service.

Jazz lets you seamlessly build, deploy and manage cloud native applications using serverless technology.
Features
  • Go Serverless in Production!
    Jazz supercharges your serverless journey while you enjoy 100% visibility into your production applications.
  • Focus on Code
    No more Infrastructure worries! Jazz comes with reusable code templates & patterns so that you can quickly turn thoughts into cool things.
  • Avoid Vendor Lock-In
    Jazz is seductively powerful & makes multi-cloud easy. You choose services from AWS, Azure, Google Cloud and others. Commission your Jazz experience, customize it to fit your needs!
  • CI/CD Solved!
    Jazz handles all the messy CI/CD work. Fall in love with Jazz and take it for a Spin today!
  • Quality Code that's Secure
    Jazz helps you focus on code quality & finds vulnerabilities before they go live!
  • Built-in Logging and Monitoring
    Troubleshoot issues and debug your serverless application quickly using live metrics and a centralized log repository.
  • Controlled Access
    You're the boss! You decide who accesses your code, approves deployments & gets access to your services.
  • Don't Go Bankrupt!
    Pay-per-use makes serverless appear insanely cheap, but it is not guaranteed. Jazz puts you in financial control so that billing mishaps are quickly avoided.
